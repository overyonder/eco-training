Reveal Solution

Valid 8-Species Selection

Seaweed, Kelp, Coral, Sea Snail, Shrimp, Crab, Small Fish, Octopus

Habitat Window

Depth: 10–25m | Temp: 20–22°C

Place at: Depth 17m, Temp 21°C

Eating Simulation

Order (by Provided): 1. Seaweed (3500) — producer 2. Kelp (3000) — producer 3. Coral (2000) — producer 4. Sea Snail (800) eats Seaweed: 3500-500=3000 ✓ 5. Octopus (750) eats Crab: 700-600=100, needs 0 more ✓ 6. Crab (700→100) eats Kelp: 3000-550=2450 ✓ 7. Eel not selected 8. Shrimp (600) eats Seaweed: 3000-400=2600 ✓ 9. Small Fish (500) eats Shrimp: 600-350=250 ✓ (Shrimp still has 250 Provided > 0) All 8 species survive. Crab survives with 100 remaining.

Why This Works

  • 3 producers provide ample calories (8500 total)
  • No apex predators with excessive calorie needs
  • Octopus eats before Crab, leaving Crab with 100 Provided
  • All terrain ranges overlap at 10–25m depth, 20–22°C
